How to configure Facebook Service

In this tutorial, you can find the instructions to import your Facebook Insights API key (access token), to configure your personal Facebook dashboard in DoUtDes. Recall to perform the following steps in strict order; you can also contact us for any problem. Also remember that you can click over any image, to zoom it.

1) Click Preferences on the sidebar and click on Continue with Facebook:

2) A pop up will appear, like below. Click on Choose your authorizations:

3) Select the page that you want to add to DoUtDes and click OK:

4) Good job! Now you have added your token in DoUtDes. Your Facebook Insights stats are now available as below:
